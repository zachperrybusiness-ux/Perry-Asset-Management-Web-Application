Macro Regime Analysis

CFA-based macro regime scoring, business cycle classification, sector rotation maps, and cross-asset confirmation using real-time economic data.

AI-Powered Macro Briefing

Generate a complete macro regime analysis using the 10-indicator CFA scorecard framework. The AI agent searches for live economic data (ISM PMI, yield curve, labor, credit conditions, monetary policy) and produces an investment-grade briefing.

Regime Scorecard

10-indicator expansion/contraction scorecard scored from live economic data. Classifies the current business cycle phase: Early Expansion, Mid-Cycle, Late Cycle, or Contraction.

Indicators: ISM Mfg PMI, ISM New Orders, Initial Claims, Yield Curve, LEI, SLOOS, HY OAS, Real FFR, NFP, Core PCE

Sector Rotation Map

Ranked sector and asset-class investment opportunity map with conviction ratings. Covers U.S. equities, tech, energy, defense, healthcare, financials, gold, TIPS, Treasuries, credit, EM, and USD.

Output: Positioning (OW/N/UW) + Conviction + Primary Thesis + Key Risk

Risk Assessment

Active macro risk scoring across five dimensions: monetary policy path, fiscal/deficit dynamics, geopolitical shocks, credit/financial stress, and growth vs. inflation trap risk.

Rated by: Severity (Low→Critical) × Duration (Transitory→Secular)

Quantitative Models

Stock decision analysis using Multiple Linear Regression, macro regime backtesting, peer comparison, and Monte Carlo simulation.

Stock Decision Tree

Enter a ticker for a full quantitative analysis: peer comparison, 10-year macro regime backtesting, MLR with bootstrapping, indicator importance ranking, and a regime-grounded 12-month investment verdict.

MLR & Indicator Ranking

Multiple Linear Regression identifies which macro and fundamental indicators have the strongest predictive power for a stock's 12-month forward return. Features include regime score, ISM PMI, yield spread, HY OAS, VIX, revenue growth, operating margin, P/E, and FCF growth.

Output: R², coefficient significance, indicator ranking by % explanatory power

Monte Carlo Simulation

1,000-path Geometric Brownian Motion simulation calibrated to historical volatility and current VIX regime. Produces probability distributions for 12-month price targets with standard deviation bands.

Output: Price distribution, confidence intervals, Sharpe-equivalent signal
