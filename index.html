About Perry Asset Management

A disciplined, research-driven approach to portfolio management — built on macro intelligence, quantitative rigor, and institutional-grade analytics.

Our Approach

Perry Asset Management combines top-down macro regime analysis with bottom-up fundamental and quantitative research. Our investment process begins with a proprietary six-pillar macro framework covering Real Economic Activity, Labor Markets, Inflation, Monetary Policy, Fiscal Policy, and International Dynamics.

Products & Services

Macro Regime Analysis

Real-time economic scorecard and sector rotation signals built on a CFA-based regime classification framework.

Portfolio Construction

Optimized allocation across equities, fixed income, alternatives, and cash — calibrated to current market conditions.

Risk Analytics

Value at Risk, Monte-Carlo stress testing, tracking error analysis, and scenario modeling across VIX and macro regimes.

Quantitative Research

ML models including regression, random forest, gradient boosting, and time-series forecasting for alpha generation.

Thematic Research

Deep dives into emerging investment themes — AI, crypto, energy transition, space — that adapt as opportunities evolve.

Fundamental & Technical Analysis

DCF valuation, ratio analysis, MACD, RSI, momentum indicators, and sector-level profitability and leverage scoring.
